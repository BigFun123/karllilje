Gravity is Particle Occlusion

Karl Lilje, 2016

Gravity is a phenomenon caused by the occlusion of particles between atoms.

There are fewer particles between atoms than outside the couplet, resulting in a relative particle vacuum between atoms. Particles push the atoms together.


This page will illustrate and demonstate WHY gravity works, and propose ideas and applications on particle occluders that can increase the velocity maxima of suborbital, superorbital, and interstellar vehicles.

Particle occlusion has implications for material research, gravitational research, and electrical design.


I am predicting a new class of materials will emerge - active materials - superalloys of superconducting metals and electrical charges to manipulate particle occlusion phenomena in realtime.

An active particle occluder material has zero apparent mass.


Photons are Assymetrical

Karl Lilje, 2020
